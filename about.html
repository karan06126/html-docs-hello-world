About US

Satyam Industries is one of the leading manufacturers of Industrial Gears in Chhattisgarh. Our products like spur gear, helical gear, double helical gear, worm wheel, gear coupling, etc are manufactured as per customer requirements. Our products are successfully used in various Industries like Bajrang Power, Real Ispat, Hira Group, Nakoda Ispat, SKS Ispat, HiTech Steel, Niros Ispat, MSP Steel and other industries of Chhattisgarh and Orissa. We are equipped with modern machinery and quality controlled at every point. The unit is managed by qualified engineers and staff. We are constantly upgrading ourselves to meet the requirements of our valuable customers. Our main aim is to provide customer satisfaction and the best quality product.
